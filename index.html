Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our bookkeeping services and how we can help your business.

What size businesses do you work with?

▼

We primarily work with small businesses across various industries. Whether you're a sole proprietor, a startup, or an established small business with multiple employees, our services can be tailored to meet your specific needs.

How often will I receive financial reports?

We typically provide monthly financial reports, but we can adjust the frequency based on your preferences. We'll work with you to determine the reporting schedule that best meets your business needs, whether that's monthly, quarterly, or another timeframe.

Do you work remotely or do I need to provide office space?

We offer both remote and on-site services, depending on your preference. Many of our clients prefer remote bookkeeping services for convenience, but we're flexible and can work on-site if that better suits your business needs.

What accounting software do you use?

We're proficient in various accounting software platforms including QuickBooks, Xero, FreshBooks, and more. We can work with your existing software or recommend a solution that best fits your business needs.

How do I get started with your services?

Getting started is easy! Simply contact us for a free consultation. We'll discuss your needs, answer your questions, and develop a customized plan for your business. Once you're ready to proceed, we'll guide you through our simple onboarding process.
